Privacy Policy

MaplePass · Last updated June 2026

Short version: we don't collect anything. MaplePass works entirely on your device and requires no account and no internet to study.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect, transmit, store on our servers, or share any personal information. We have no servers. We use no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

Your study data

Everything you do in the app — quiz results, flashcards, streaks, checklists, and settings — is stored only on your device. It never leaves your phone. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

Subscriptions & payments

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never receive or see your payment details. Apple's handling of your purchase is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above.
