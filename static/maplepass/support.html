Frequently asked questions

Is there a free version?

Yes. You can use Mixed Practice, the first practice part, the Matching game, a sample flashcard deck and one Study chapter for free. A subscription unlocks all practice, mock exams, every flashcard deck, the full study guide and analytics.

What are the subscription terms?

Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Review our Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

How does the free trial work?

New subscribers get a 1-day free trial. You won't be charged if you cancel before the trial ends. Subscriptions renew automatically until cancelled.

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

On your iPhone: Settings ▸ [your name] ▸ Subscriptions ▸ MaplePass, or open the App Store, tap your photo, then Subscriptions. Cancelling stops future renewals; you keep access until the end of the current period.

I paid but features are locked. How do I restore?

Open the app ▸ Settings (gear on Home) ▸ Restore purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID used to subscribe.

Does it work offline?

Completely. All questions, the official guide and your progress are stored on your device. No internet is required to study.

Are the "current facts" (Prime Minister, premier, mayor…) up to date?

Yes — they're built in and dated, so the app shows the right answer for today automatically and refreshes with each app update. Always confirm office-holders close to your test date.

How do I reset my progress?

Deleting and reinstalling the app clears all on-device data. (A dedicated reset option can be added on request.)

Is my data collected?

No. We don't collect, track or share any personal data. See our Privacy Policy.
